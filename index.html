    GPGTools is a collection of several softwares bringing encryption/decryption and signing of e-mails and files to you on your mac (see Gpg4win if you use Windows).The main goal is to bring OpenPGP to Apple OS X in the form of an easy installer package based on MacGPG. Read the introduction to get a detailed idea of how PGP works.


    The project section provides more information about the included applications and related projects.
