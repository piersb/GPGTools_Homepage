Overview

GPGMail is an open source plugin for Apple Mail. It brings the functionality to sign, verify, encrypt and decrypt mails using the OpenPGP standard.

Please have a look at the Knowledge Base and GPGMail FAQ-Section. It would be nice to tell Apple to support bundles for Mail.app officially.



Support and Issues

Please have a look at the list of open issues and ask on our support page if you need further help.

Often reported limitations:

  • Currently GPGMail for Lion is an early alpha version under development.
